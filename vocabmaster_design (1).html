Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

Go to the login screen and tap "Forgot password?". Enter your email address and we'll send you a reset link.

How does the spaced repetition work?

Our AI algorithm shows you words at optimal intervals based on your performance, helping you remember them long-term.

Can I use the app offline?

Yes! Premium users can download content for offline use. Your progress will sync when you're back online.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to Settings > Subscription > Manage Subscription. You can cancel anytime and keep access until the end of your billing period.

What's the difference between learning modes?

Smart Cards use spaced repetition, Story Mode teaches in context, Memory Palace uses visual association, and Quiz Mode tests your knowledge.
